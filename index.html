About Us

Our Vision

**GDJPS**, a **CBSE-affiliated** school established in **2023**. Our aim is to prepare children holistically through education, moral development, and the use of modern technology.

We strive to develop a sense of knowledge, self-confidence, and responsibility in children, preparing them to be future leaders. We believe in nurturing both academic excellence and ethical values.

Class Details

Pre-Primary Wing (Nursery - UKG)

Focus on foundational skills, sensory development, and playful learning methods. Small batch sizes for personalized attention.

Primary Wing (Class I - V)

Introduction to core subjects, activity-based curriculum, and emphasis on reading, writing, and logical thinking.

Middle Wing (Class VI - VIII)

Detailed subject study, project-based learning, and preparation for advanced academic challenges under the CBSE structure.
